How to play

Basic

  • Before starting a race, choose your character by selecting "Select Uma" on the right side of the screen.
  • There are many characters availableâ€”pick your favorite to compete with.
  • You can view each character's base speed stats by clicking the "->" button on the left side of the screen.
  • Once you've made your choice, close the panel by pressing "Done" at the bottom.
  • Then select "Start Race" to begin racing with your chosen character.
  • Note: You cannot start a race unless you have selected a character.

Race

  • You will compete against 6 other characters on a track that is 8000 pixels long.
  • Every character receives a random speed boost between 0.0-0.9 every five seconds during the race.
  • Your chosen character will activate their unique skill to boost speed at a random time between 70-100 seconds after the race begins.
  • One of your opponens will also activate their unique skill within the same time range.
  • Each skill lasts for 10 seconds and can only be used once per race.
